- 工具方法

  - bind

  - merge

- axios使用方式

  - `axios(option)`

    ```javascript
    axios({url, method, headers})
    ```

  - `axios(url[,options])`

    ```javascript
    axios(url, {method, headers,})
    ```

  - `axios[method](url[,option])`

  - `axios[method](url[,data[,option]])`

  - `axios.request(option)`

    ```javascript
    axios.request({
    	url,
    	method,
    	headers,
    })
    ```
